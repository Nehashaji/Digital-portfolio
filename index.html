About Me

I’m Neha Shaji, a final-year BSc Creative Computing student at Bath Spa University RAK. My academic journey blends creativity with technology, giving me the ability to design, develop, and deliver impactful digital experiences.

Over the years, I have worked on a variety of projects, with a strong focus on web development, while also exploring game design, mobile applications, chatbots, and data visualizations. This hands-on experience has shaped me into a versatile creator who can adapt to different domains and think beyond conventional boundaries.

Looking ahead, I am preparing for an MSc in Data Science & Artificial Intelligence, where I aim to strengthen my expertise in data analysis, machine learning, and AI-driven solutions. My goal is to grow into a Data Analyst who can transform complex data into meaningful insights, combining technical depth with a creative perspective to solve real-world problems.
